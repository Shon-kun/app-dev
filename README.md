## 1. Shadow Garden
  - Genre: Action, Fantasy, Comedy
    
  - Description:  "Shadow Garden" follows the story of a young man named Cid who aspires to become a powerful "shadow" and work behind the scenes to protect the world from hidden threats. He pretends to be an average person, but secretly leads a group called "Shadow Garden," made up of people who think they're fighting a secret organization, unaware that it's all part of Cid's fantasy. The series blends comedy, action, and fantasy with a focus on Cid's exaggerated sense of self-importance.

## 2. Naruto
  - Genre: Action, Adventure, Ninja, Superpower
    
  - Description: "Naruto" follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and the leader of his village (Hokage). Orphaned and ostracized for having a powerful demon fox sealed within him, Naruto strives to gain recognition and friendship. The series explores his growth, friendships, and the challenges he faces as he seeks to protect his loved ones and face powerful enemies.

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/47029729-4c9e-4150-8711-e530f2978581" width="1200" height="600">

## 3. Your Lie in April
  - Genre: Drama, Romance, Music
    
  - Description:  "Your Lie in April" tells the story of Kousei Arima, a piano prodigy who loses his ability to hear the sound of the piano after his mother's death. His life changes when he meets a spirited violinist named Kaori Miyazono, who brings music back into his life. A deeply emotional series that explores themes of love, loss, and healing, "Your Lie in April" is both a beautiful coming-of-age story and a heart-wrenching drama.

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9d148ca9-6686-4b05-9f37-96efc021d102" width="1200" height="600">

## 4. I  Want to Eat Your Pancreas
  - Genre: Drama, Romance
    
  - Description:  "I Want to Eat Your Pancreas" is a touching and emotional anime film about a high school boy who learns that a classmate, Sakura Yamauchi, has a terminal pancreatic disease. Despite her illness, Sakura lives her life with a positive attitude, and the two form an unlikely bond. The story focuses on themes of life, death, and the fleeting nature of relationships, with a poignant exploration of human connections.

## 5. The Angel Next Door Spoils Me Rotten
  - Genre: Romance, Slice of Life, Comedy
    
  - Description:  "The Angel Next Door Spoils Me Rotten" is a sweet and heartwarming romance that follows the story of Amane Fujimiya, a high school student who lives next door to the perfect and popular girl, Mahiru Shiina. After Amane falls ill and Mahiru helps him, they form an unlikely friendship, with Mahiru gradually spoiling and caring for Amane in a gentle, teasing manner. It's a charming series about growing affection, daily life, and emotional support.

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c831f7ec-4817-4bd2-8e8a-12d6434f9dc2" width="1200" height="600">

## 6. My Love Story with Yamada-kun at Lv999
  - Genre: Romance, Comedy, Fantasy
    
  - Description:  "My Love Story with Yamada-kun at Lv999" follows the story of Akane, a young girl who is heartbroken after being dumped by her boyfriend while playing a popular online game. She meets Yamada, a mysterious and high-level player in the same game, who also happens to be an introverted gamer. As they bond over their shared love of gaming, their relationship grows into something deeper and more meaningful, blending romance and comedy with gaming culture.
